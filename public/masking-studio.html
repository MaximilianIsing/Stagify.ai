How the Masking Studio works

  1. Upload a photo of your room.
  2. Highlight where each piece of furniture should go. Use one color per area, and add more areas for more pieces.
  3. Give every area a furniture photo, a short prompt, or both.
  4. Click Apply Edit. Every area is staged at the same time, and only the highlighted spots can change. The rest of your photo stays pixel-perfect.

Keyboard shortcuts

B brush · E erase · [ ] brush size · 1–6 pick area · Ctrl+Z undo · hold H view photo

Ctrl+scroll to zoom, hold Space (or the middle mouse button) to pan

Discard staged results?

Starting over with a new photo clears your highlighted areas and staged results.
