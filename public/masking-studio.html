How the Masking Studio works

  1. Upload a photo of your room.
  2. Highlight where each piece of furniture should go: paint it, drag a rectangle, or use the Magic tool to select an object or area with one click. Use one color per area.
  3. Give every area a furniture photo, a short prompt, or both. Or switch an area to Remove object to clear out whatever it covers.
  4. Click Apply Edit. Every area is staged at the same time, and only the highlighted spots can change. The rest of your photo stays pixel-perfect.
  5. Not happy with an area? Use Try another version to generate more options and flip between them.

Keyboard shortcuts
