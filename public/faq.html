Frequently Asked Questions

What is virtual staging?

Virtual staging is the process of digitally furnishing a property photo to showcase its potential, helping buyers envision how a space can look and feel when fully styled. Unlike traditional staging, it's faster, more cost-effective, and allows for multiple design options tailored to different buyer preferences. With high-quality visuals, virtual staging highlights the property's best features, making it easier for buyers to connect emotionally and imagine themselves living there.

How long does it take?

On average, virtual staging takes around 8 seconds per photo to process, though the exact time can be a little shorter or longer depending on the complexity of the space and the design style chosen. The result is a quick, seamless transformation that makes your property photos stand out.

How do I select a custom theme?

If you don't see the theme you want, you can select "Custom" and then add a specific prompt for the theme you want. We'll use your prompt to generate the theme you want.
