Frequently Asked Questions

What is virtual staging?

Virtual staging is the process of digitally furnishing a property photo to showcase its potential, helping buyers envision how a space can look and feel when fully styled. Unlike traditional staging, it's faster, more cost-effective, and allows for multiple design options tailored to different buyer preferences. With high-quality visuals, virtual staging highlights the property's best features, making it easier for buyers to connect emotionally and imagine themselves living there.

How long does it take?

On average, virtual staging takes around 15 seconds per photo to process, though the exact time can be a little shorter or longer depending on the complexity of the space and the design style chosen. The result is a quick, seamless transformation that makes your property photos stand out.

Do you remove existing furniture?

This demo doesn't edit or remove existing objects—it's a quick mock-up designed to validate the flow and showcase the concept. The final product will deliver full editing capabilities for a polished, professional result.
