Why it works

Buyers shop with their eyes first

Almost every buyer starts online, so your listing photos are the real first showing. Empty or dated rooms are hard to read; people can't judge scale or picture living there. Staging gives each room a clear purpose and a pull, which is why agents have always relied on it.

  • Empty rooms photograph smaller and colder than they are
  • Staging shows the intended use of every space
  • Stronger photos earn more clicks, saves, and showings
How it works

From empty photo to listing-ready in ~8 seconds

Upload a photo, pick the room type and one of seven styles, and Stagify furnishes it in about eight seconds. Want changes? Remove the existing furniture, add a custom prompt, or mask one area to adjust it without starting over.

  • 7 styles plus custom prompts and furniture removal
  • Mask-edit any part of a result without redoing it
  • Full copyright, yours to use on the MLS or in ads

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most. Still stuck? Visit Guides or get in touch.

What is virtual staging?

Virtual staging is the process of digitally furnishing a property photo to showcase its potential, helping buyers envision how a space can look and feel when fully styled.

How long does it take?

On average, virtual staging takes around 8 seconds per photo to process.

How do I select a custom theme?

If you don't see the theme you want, you can select "Custom" and then add a specific prompt for the theme you want.

What makes an ideal room for staging?

An ideal room for staging should have good lighting, clear walls, and minimal clutter.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your uploaded images are processed securely and are not stored permanently on our servers.
